Hard Tech Derivatives (2026) Stage 2 Explorer (2025)

My first actual Touhou album! Every remix here takes huge inspiration from existing songs in one way or another (some are nearly complete copies!), hence the name. Why did I do such a thing? Primarily, I saw this as a learning experience-- and sure enough, I learned plenty of sound design trying to recreate many of the original sounds. Second, I lack skill in arrangement, and it is far easier to copy someone else :P. Lastly, and probably most honorably, I see this as a way of paying respect to my favorite music. While some of the original circles are disbanded, or some of the tracks never got popular, I hope my homage will allow their forgotten voices to be heard once again. Favorite: Crew 66
