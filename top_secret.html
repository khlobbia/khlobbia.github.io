Captain's Cringe Corner.

A collection of some of my music over the years :

One thing I want to say is: I've tried so many different styles over the years, and there are so many interesting tracks I've made. However, if I included every single unique idea I had, this list would be far too long for anyone to endure. Thus, I'm only currently showing the tracks that mark a pivotal moment in my journey. Of course, if you'd like to hear more old tracks, just let me know :)

Dance Like a Cossack - Feb 6, 2023

My first ever track, made on Bandlab! And no, I never had a natural talent for music-- what did you expect? :P

Decoration Battle Hardbass Remix - Feb 7, 2023

My first remix! Made on day 2! All I did was import a touhou midi, replace a few instruments, and add a beat. That's all. And it. Is. Painful. (I really did not know what an offbeat was XD). The next track I made on day 3 was extraordinary, however I don't want to list too many.

guitar bass jam - Feb 25, 2023

When I made this, I thought "this is good!" But yeah. That's just under a month of experience.

full hardbass - Jul 22, 2023

My first time on FL Studio (I think)! After about half a year on Bandlab, I went and got the trial version of FL. At the time, all I knew how to use were samples and soundfonts (of which I somehow only had a Touhou PC-98 soundfont). But, this turned out all right at the time. Unfortunately, I didn't seem to understand that the lead and bass should use the same notes...

コンクリート2 - Oct 7, 2024

About 1.5 years after my first track, this was made. Despite being rather basic techno, I was very proud of it, and it also marked the moment that my music was finally starting to be "coherent." Something special about this track is that, besides the vocal samples, ALL the sounds (percussion and bass etc) were made from scratch in synthesizers!

Laidback Lento, Flurry! - May 4, 2026

And well, you know where I am today :). After three years and one hundred tracks, I'm quite happy with where I am. As a bonus, here's my latest track, a videogame-styled theme!
